## Electronic Signatures (Template)
Users agree that electronic signatures created through the platform may be used as evidence of intent to sign, subject to applicable law.

## Evidence Package (Template)
The platform may generate an evidence package and/or certificate summarizing signing events and cryptographic hashes.

## Retention & Deletion (Template)
The platform retains documents and evidence for plan-defined periods, subject to legal hold and applicable law.

## Plan Limits (Template)
Subscription plans may include limits (e.g., monthly document quota, storage quota). Enforcement behavior (warnings, throttling, hard stops) must be defined and disclosed.

## Required Local Changes
- Align with jurisdictional e-signature law and consumer disclosure requirements.
- Align with privacy law requirements (PII processing, retention, deletion, access).
- Align with professional responsibility rules (law firm data handling).
